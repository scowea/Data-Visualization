Media Maps

This map identifies all the different media Obama hope has been used as. Too often obama hope is categorized as an internet meme. I wanted to show that it has a material body that is distributed, which is why I think it needs to be understood as a body multiple. To emphasize its embodiment, I created a digital sculpture of the Obama Hope, which you can click on to see how many different embodiments Obama Hope has come to have.
